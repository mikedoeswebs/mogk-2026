On the 6th of March 2023, the first Club MO/GK session took place at Halstead Leisure Centre in Essex. Nine young goalkeepers came along to our first session, four of whom are still training with us today.

Since then, we've grown to a twenty-strong group of children aged from 7 to 14, who show up every week to learn key goalkeeping skills, make friends and, most importantly for us, have fun.

On the 11th of March 2024, we celebrated our first anniversary as a group with a bumper turnout and a session focusing on dealing with cutbacks and low balls close to the goal.

Thanks to all the players who show up, parents, grandparents, and carers who bring them each week, and to my awesome coaching team for helping us offer something I am immensely proud of. Here's to the next twelve months!
