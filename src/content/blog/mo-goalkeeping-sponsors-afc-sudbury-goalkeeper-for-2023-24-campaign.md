**MO Goalkeeping is proud to be sponsoring [AFC Sudbury Women](https://x.com/AFCSudburyWomen)‘s goalkeeper Millie Carter for the 2023/24 campaign.**

AFC Sudbury are competing in the FA Women’s National League this season for the first time in the club’s history and Millie, who progressed through the club’s academy system, will be charged with keeping out the likes of Norwich City, Queen’s Park Rangers, Cambridge United & AFC Wimbledon out.

I have made it my mission to support and promote goalkeepers and goalkeeping in the local area in whatever way possible, whether that's through [specialist private goalkeeper coaching](/goalkeeper-coaching/), working with clubs to help train their 'keepers, helping goalkeepers find teams, or helping teams find goalkeepers. Player sponsorship is just the latest way that MO Goalkeeping is helping to strengthen goalkeeping in and around Suffolk.
